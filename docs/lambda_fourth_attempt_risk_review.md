# Lambda Fourth-Attempt Risk Review

The fourth-attempt risk review treats another real launch as high risk after
three response-loss outcomes. A future fourth attempt requires:

- all prior incidents closed,
- crash-safe diagnostics accepted,
- endpoint confidence reviewed,
- fresh read-only discovery,
- fresh operator approval,
- no automatic launch retry,
- owned-only termination and read-only termination verification.

M035 does not clear these requirements into execution authority.
