# Lambda Final Budget Lock

The M028 final budget lock freezes first-launch spend constraints for M029
review.

Required limits:
- maximum budget: 50 USD
- maximum runtime: 30 minutes
- maximum instances: 1
- planned hours: at most 0.5
- safety buffer included
- selected price record present
- projected remaining credits nonnegative

The lock does not spend money and does not enable launch.
