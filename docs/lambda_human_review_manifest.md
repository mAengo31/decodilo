# Lambda Human Review Manifest

The M026 human review manifest records a reviewer's explicit acknowledgement
that M026 can only authorize M027 implementation planning.

The manifest records hashes of reviewed artifacts, the requested M026 decision,
and acknowledgement booleans for:
- future billable action risk
- no launch in M026
- M027 code-only scope
- separate M028 launch approval
- termination and read-only verification requirements
- OS shutdown being insufficient
- one instance, 30 minutes, and $50 defaults
- no training, SSH, setup scripts, or unattended work

Completion does not enable launch. Limits above one instance, 30 minutes, or
$50 block validation. Missing acknowledgements block validation.
