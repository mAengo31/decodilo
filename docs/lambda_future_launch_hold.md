# Lambda Future Launch Hold

The future-launch hold prevents another launch attempt while an M031 incident is
open or while repeated response loss remains unmitigated.

The hold requires:

- M031 incident closeout;
- repeated response-loss review;
- accepted root-cause mitigation;
- fresh operator approval before any later launch review.

Even when the hold clears, it does not authorize launch by itself. A future
launch still needs the full budget, ownership, read-only discovery, operator,
endpoint policy, mutation guard, and termination-verification gates.
