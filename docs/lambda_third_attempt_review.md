# Lambda Third Attempt Review

M033 is a review-only package for a future M034 third one-instance launch
attempt. It consumes the M031 incident closeout and M032 response-loss
mitigation evidence, but it does not launch, terminate, mutate, or spend.

The review requires:

- endpoint-spec operator confirmation;
- response-capture settings lock;
- launch timeout policy with no automatic launch retry;
- third-attempt risk review;
- third-attempt correlation plan;
- third-attempt reconciliation plan;
- renewed operator approval;
- fresh read-only discovery and budget/resource checks for the future attempt.

The highest positive M033 outcome is
`go_for_future_m034_third_launch_review`. It is not launch approval.
`launch_ready=false` and `launch_allowed=false` remain enforced.
